Settings

News comes from Google News (Moneycontrol, Economic Times, LiveMint, Business Standard and others) — no account or API key needed. Your stock list is stored only on this device.
Accepts .xlsx, .xls, or .csv. Needs 3 columns: ticker, company name, tier (Top30 / Top31-50 / Top51-75 / Watch). Header row optional. Also auto-detects Kite Holdings export — sets all to Watch tier.
Your backend (required for news + Kite login)
News fetching and Kite login both go through a small backend you host for free (Render or Railway) — this avoids the free anonymous proxies this app used before, which started failing for everyone (rate-limits and anti-abuse blocks on the underlying services, not specific to this app). See kite_backend/README.md in the project files for a 10-minute setup if you haven't done this yet.
Import from Kite (Zerodha) — optional
Enter your Kite Connect API key and tap the button. After you log in on Zerodha's site, you'll be redirected back here and your holdings will be fetched automatically. Uses the backend URL set above.
